About HopeHands

Our mission is to create lasting change through compassion, transparency, and community empowerment.

Our Story

A Journey of Compassion

Founded in 2010, HopeHands began with a simple idea: that every person deserves access to basic necessities — education, healthcare, clean water, and opportunity. What started as a small group of passionate volunteers has grown into a global movement.

Today, we operate in 45 countries, partnering with local communities to design and implement sustainable solutions. Our approach is rooted in transparency — every rupee you donate is tracked and reported.

Our Journey

Milestones Through the Years

2010

Foundation Established

HopeHands was founded in Mumbai with a team of 5 volunteers and a dream to change the world.

2013

First 1,000 Lives Impacted

Our education program reached its first major milestone, providing schooling to 1,000 children in rural India.

2016

Global Expansion

Expanded operations to 15 countries across Asia, Africa, and South America with healthcare and water projects.

2020

COVID Relief Campaign

Raised ₹5 Cr to provide food, medical supplies, and financial support to families affected by the pandemic.

2024

15,000+ Lives Transformed

Crossed the milestone of impacting 15,000 lives with 250+ funded projects across 45 countries.
